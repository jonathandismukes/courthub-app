CourtHub - Firebase Hosting Deployment Guide
=============================================

Your support page is now ready at /support.html

DEPLOYMENT STEPS:
-----------------

1. Deploy using Dreamflow's Publish button (top-right corner)
   OR
   Run this command in your terminal:
   
   cd /path/to/your/project
   firebase deploy --only hosting

2. After deployment, you'll get a Firebase Hosting URL like:
   https://your-project-id.web.app/
   OR
   https://your-project-id.firebaseapp.com/

3. Your support page will be accessible at:
   https://your-project-id.web.app/support.html

4. Update App Store Connect:
   - Go to App Store Connect
   - Navigate to your app → App Information
   - Update the Support URL field with:
     https://your-project-id.web.app/support.html
   - Save changes
   - Resubmit your app for review
