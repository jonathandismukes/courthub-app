About CourtHub

CourtHub helps you find basketball, pickleball, and tennis courts near you with real-time player counts. Whether you're looking for an empty court or want to join an active game, CourtHub makes it easy to discover and check in to courts in your area.

Features

  • Interactive map showing nearby courts for basketball, pickleball, and tennis
  • Real-time player counts for each court
  • Browse as guest with limited features; create a free account for full access
  • Check in to courts and let others know you're playing
  • View court details, ratings, and reviews
  • Save your favorite courts
  • Connect with friends and create pickup games
  • Get notifications when friends are playing nearby

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do I need an account to use CourtHub?
A: You can browse as a guest with limited features (nearby and search). To check in, save favorites, connect with friends, and get notifications, create a free account for full access.
Q: How do I find nearby courts?
A: Open the app and navigate to the "Map" tab. You'll see all available parks with basketball, pickleball, and tennis courts in your vicinity.
Q: How do I check in to a court?
A: Select a park from the map or home page, then choose a specific court. Tap the "Check In" button and enter the number of players.
Q: How does real-time player count work?
A: Users check in to courts when they arrive, and the player count updates automatically. When they leave, they can check out to update the count for others.
Q: What if a court's player count is incorrect?
A: You can update the player count for any court you are checked into. Simply go to the park detail page, select the court, and adjust the number.
Q: Why does CourtHub need my location?
A: We use your location to show you nearby courts on the map and help you find the closest places to play. Your location is only used while you're using the app and is never shared with other users without your permission.
Q: Is CourtHub free?
A: Yes, the core features are free to use. We may offer optional paid upgrades in the future to enhance your experience.
Q: How do I report a bug or suggest a feature?
A: Please email us at silverstreak622000@yahoo.com with a detailed description of the issue or your feature request.
Q: Can I add a new court that's not on the map?
A: Currently, courts are sourced from public databases. If you'd like to request a new court be added, please email us with the court name and location.

Privacy

CourtHub respects your privacy. We only collect information necessary to provide our services:

  • Location data is used only to show nearby courts
  • Your email is used for account management and notifications
  • Profile information is only visible to users you connect with
  • We never sell your data to third parties

Technical Support

If you're experiencing technical issues:

  • Make sure you have the latest version of the app
  • Check your internet connection
  • Try logging out and logging back in
  • If the issue persists, email us with details about the problem
